About the Page

A clearer home for school life and community updates.

This community page began as a student-led online space and now works best when it acts as a reliable school-facing platform for updates, celebration, and connection.

Origin

Started on April 1, 2024

The original page was founded by Ricky and supported by a small team of admins, content creators, and a web developer. Its early purpose was to share light content and school-related moments.

The stronger direction for the site is now clear: become a quality digital home for the school community, with better presentation, easier reading, and clearer priorities.

Current direction

The site now centers school identity first, with a tone that is respectful, warm, and community-minded.

  • Share official-looking event stories and school updates.
  • Help students and families find contact and location details quickly.
  • Keep space for culture and special reading features without weakening the brand.
Core Values

What the page should communicate.

A school community site succeeds when its design and content support trust, pride, and participation.

Respect

Teachers, students, and families should be represented with dignity in both visual design and writing tone.

Belonging

The page should feel open and welcoming to the full community, not only a small group of insiders.

Clarity

Information should be easy to read, easy to navigate, and strong enough to support regular updates over time.

What you will find

Content areas

Community news

Event coverage, school ceremonies, and notable updates.

Campus information

Addresses and basic contact guidance in a clean, direct format.

Special reading

Cultural and seasonal features that add personality to the site.

“Design quality changes how seriously a community page is taken.”

That is why the site now uses stronger hierarchy, better spacing, mobile navigation, and consistent page structure.
