Why this page matters

One school page should feel calm, clear, and useful.

The site now focuses on school identity instead of novelty. It presents updates in a structured way, supports both English and Myanmar content, and keeps important information easy to find on every device.

  • Clear navigation for news, campus details, and contact.
  • Cleaner reading layouts for articles and event coverage.
  • A visual style that feels academic, warm, and community-driven.
“A school website should not just publish information. It should strengthen connection.”

The community page is designed to do that through updates, recognition, and shared memory.
